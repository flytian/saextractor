Overview

Ontology-based Visual Analytics For Text Analysis

This is a MSc Project of Tianwei Dong

Topic

With the increasing of data on the web, it is challenging to capture and present data meaningfully. In this project, we will study the use of ontology to assist in data capture and visualisation. In particularly, we will use visualisation to facilitate an active learning process in developing and evolving an ontology.
We will use seminar announcements as a case study. Most seminar announcements are communicated through the web, but they are often structured very differently and exhibit a variety of forms. At Oxford, there are numerous seminars each weak. We will

  • Develop a web crawler for collecting such textual data.
  • Develop an ontology for representing concepts in seminar announcements and their relationships.
  • Develop an ontology-based algorithm for extracting seminar information from captured web text.
  • Use visualisation to monitor the extraction process, and to diagnose errors or incompleteness in the ontology that may have lead to the extraction errors.
  • Use interaction to facilitate active learning of the ontology.
  • Create a visualisation for showing the collected seminar information from different sources.
